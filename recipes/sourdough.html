Sourdough Bread Recipe

Recipe to make the perfect sourdough loaf

After many breakfasts and dinners accompanied by fresh sourdough, I found myself descending into a full obsession with how to make sourdough bread. There was something ancient about performing the whole process that I found captivating. It was–and still is–exciting to mix such elemental ingredients and to see them produce beautiful, life-giving sustenance: it is modern-day alchemy. It’s such a simple thing, and yet it brought me so much joy to see my family and friends tear into a freshly baked loaf that I wanted to bake every day of every week.

Ingredients

  • white bread flour
  • whole whear flour
  • fine sea salt
  • water

Steps

  1. Levian
  2. Autolyse
  3. Mix
  4. Bulk Fermentation
  5. Divide and Preshape
  6. Shape
  7. Rest and Proof
  8. Bake
